About Our School

Calapan Chu Eng School is a private school that offers Preschool to 6th Grade Education with Chinese and English classes and accepts Students of any race. It was founded in 1930, rebuilt in 1985 with the new building constructed in 2017 and it is located at Araceli Luna St., Barangay Ilaya, Calapan, Oriental Mindoro, Philippines. The current principal is Ms. Shareen Grace C. Ganchua. Currently catering 250 preschoolers and elementary students.
